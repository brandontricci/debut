About Us

Santa Barbara Design Studio is an industry leader in the design, manufacture and wholesale distribution of licensed art gifts, home decor, fashion accessories and entertainware products to the mid-tier and upscale retail channels.

Founded by Ray Markow in 1976 as a ceramics studio to produce his original designs, the company discovered the power of licensing in 1994 through its original partnership with greeting card artist Mary Engelbreit. In the years since that groundbreaking licensing success, the company has shown consistent growth by discovering and partnering with exciting artists and product developers — serving as their sales, marketing, distribution and manufacturing arms — as well as utilizing the Studio's talented in-house designers to bring new products to market.

Financially sound and consistently profitable, SBDS owns a 57,000 sq. ft. building in Southern California which houses its corporate headquarters, design staff, innovative digital printing studio, as well as a state-of-the-art warehouse and distribution center. The company¹s office in Shanghai employs a staff to locate top factories and provide complete inspections of all manufacturing.

The company has earned an enviable reputation with retailers for bringing the most trend-forward and salable products to market. Its reputation as a top-selling company is evidenced by its consistent placement on the top of Gift Beat¹s best-seller charts, as well as its nomination by LIMA in 2011 as Art Licensee of the Year for the Lolita glassware collection.

Owner Ray Markow attributes the company¹s success to his dedicated staff of  industry veterans who share his a passion for being the best in the business  by adhering to the highest ethical standards, and furthering the company¹s  reputation for showcasing the newest and most exciting products in the  industry.
