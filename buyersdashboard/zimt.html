About

Hi There,

Thanks for stopping by! I’m glad you found the Zimt site- based on the page you’re at, you’d probably like to know a little bit more about Zimt. Here we go-

For as long as I can remember, I’ve has had a passion for ethical living. My passion has become my full-time career, as I’m the owner and founder of raw chocolate company, Zimt Artisan Chocolates.

In the spring of 2010, I set out to backpack around Europe after having finished my courses at a university in Germany. And what kept coming up along that path? Raw chocolate! This delicious treat was already in Europe- and proving to be much loved. I was in my favourite cafe in London, right before the real trek around the continent was to begin and was gifted with a box of beautiful raw truffles. For no reason whatsoever or so I thought at the time. (Maybe I just looked hungry?).

Fast forward a few months. I was back in Canada, and working to finish up at university. But finishing up school meant starting up real life- and what would that look like? I’d always wanted to have my own business and now seemed like a great time- no kids to be responsible for, no mortgage, apparently enough energy for this sort of thing… sounds like a plan! Now, to figure out a business to start. Well, might as well make it raw chocolate- Europe seems to love it and it certainly backs my beliefs. (Some may argue that creating a chocolate company is anything but starting up “real life” but… it’s the closest version I can muster.) Starting on the path to create a truly delicious raw chocolate meant it was time to start experimenting- now, I’m not the best in the kitchen, and chocolate is not an easy feat to master! But, with a lot of practice (and a lot of taste testing), recipes were formulated and the real challenge began- learning (and still learning) about the business side of things. Slowly but surely, Zimt was taking shape. After a handful of all nighters in the kitchen, thinking a little bit more strategically, being a little less stubborn about how this business should grow, and tons of help from friends and family, Zimt is enjoyed by many! And for good reason- these are some tasty treats. All cacao ingredients are raw and only coconut sugar is used to sweeten the chocolate and all products. Fillers, emulsifiers, and unpronounceable ingredients are nowhere to be seen!

I’m is looking forward to turning more people onto the world of a whole foods, plant based lifestyle- and knows that chocolate is a great way to make that happen. Thanks again for checking in! Looking forward to bringing you more tasty and happy treats.

Emma
