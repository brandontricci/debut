About Us

Health Science Labs helps keep families healthy with its high quality nutritional supplements, first aid and other eco-friendly products for kids and babies that Moms can trust.

First Aid

Nothing comforts a child better or relieves the boo boo or fever faster while keeping your fingers warm than our reusable animal character cold pack. Additionally, our 73 piece Rescue first aid kit and Organic Boo Boo balm with Arnica are must haves for any medicine cabinet. Our safety-harness backpack keeps your child within reach while the adorable animal backpack stores your child’s favorite items.

Bath-time

Through our quality Endangered Species by SudSmart bath-time products, such as bath coloring mats with "no-roll" soap crayons, bath mitts and animal scrubbies, you can be sure your kids will be getting clean while they play & learn more about endangered species.

Health Science Labs is committed to being environmentally friendly. Our Endangered Species bath sets, Eco-Doodle and Eco-Labels use recycled packaging and renewable resources such as biodegradable "stone paper" printed with soy ink that doesn’t kill tress, doesn’t pollute the air or water, and saves energy and water. All of our liquid bath products are paraben, sulfate and dye-free.

Endangered Wildlife donations

Every purchase of an Endangered Species by HSL product helps endangered animals through our donations to the Defenders of Wildlife Organization. http://www.defenders.org/
