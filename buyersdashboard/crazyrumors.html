OUR STORY

Two Words - Naturally Fun!

That's what Crazy Rumors is all about! A lip treat unlike any other! The purest, cleanest ingredients found on earth fused with deliciously fun flavors!

So who are you anyway?

Crazy Rumors is Edie and Brian Himmel - a fun loving, creative couple who took a leap of faith and decided to do what we truly love!

How it all started

Back in 2003 while living in Brooklyn, NY - we took a look around and what we didn't see really excited us! There were tons of lip balms out there, but they were all missing that one thing - that extra coolness factor that separated the ordinary from the extraordinary! There were two options: simple and plain, harboring the same basic flavors and scents, or wild and over the top but made of purely synthetic ingredients!

And off we go

Relentlessly seeking out the perfect lip balm recipe with no luck, we decided to create one on our own! Formulated from the ground up with one main goal: to create the best all natural & vegan lip balm-- dare we say-- in the world!

Eureka!

After months upon months of late night experiments and testing, we were pretty certain we had done it! Not only did our lips feel astonishing but our kitchen smelled amazing! In the famous words of Suze Orman APPROVED!

What we made

All Natural & Vegan, indulgently clean, out of this world flavored lip treats made with certified organic ingredients - like super moisturizing organic shea butter and soothing organic jojoba oil. Infused with only the best naturally derived flavors & pure essential oils, and slightly sweetened with just a hint of natural stevia, so our lip treats not only smell amazing, they taste great too!

Our gorgeous packaging, amazing flavors and to die for scents are truly a breath of fresh air! Your lips will feel soft and fabulous, and you will feel good about what you put onto your body!
