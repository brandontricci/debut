THE STORY

WE RUN ON NUT BUTTERS.

THERE, WE’VE SAID IT.

SPOONFULS PER DAY.

In fact, nut butters have fueled our bodies for as long as we can remember. As one of those perfect foods packed with protein, healthy fats, and a wide range of micronutrients, we’ve consumed hundreds...actually, thousands... of pounds, pumping out wholesome sustained energy for the everyday grind.

Over time, our love for fresh, healthy food led us to grind our own homemade batches. In an instant we were hooked. When we met in 2011, we bonded over the delicacies each of us were crafting individually and our desire to improve the lives of others. So, we set forth to expand the fledgling Yumbutter brand into a company that could have monumental impact.

YUMBUTTER GO

MT. EVEREST?

ALL YOU NEED IS PB.

Fueled by the goodness of nut butters, we’ve traveled the world, we’ve started our own businesses, hiked, biked, and run, and never met a rope swing we didn’t like. Heck, peanut butter even fueled Adrian’s trek to Mt. Everest Base Camp and back.

We’ve hauled jars of nut butters around the world and around town. Winter camping in northern Wisconsin? You betcha. Hiking in Colorado. Yep. Biking country roads and city streets. All the time. It finally occurred to us that we could make this lighter & more portable, and more nutritious for our everyday adventures.

YumbutterGO is born and we decide to launch it to the world using Kickstarter. Backers bought over 1000 pouches in 30 days and we knew we were on to something big. We’ve worked day and night to bring you the ONLY:

Organic GO-Anywhere peanut butter

Superfood integrated nut butter

Social impact driven nut butter
