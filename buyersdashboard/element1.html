About Us

Our Product Philosophy and Information

Element Botanicals is located in beautiful White Lake, BC, the heart of the Canadian Shuswap. We produce each product by hand in small batches with the best available ingredients, supporting organically grown and locally sourced suppliers as often as possible.

Our products are available coast to coast; from Dartmouth, Nova Scotia to Vancouver Island, British Columbia and many points in between. We also supply Spas with bulk treatments to give their clients the highest quality services. We have been providing innovative body care products with cutting edge design for 8 years with a satisfaction guaranty. Spoiler alert: No one complains.

We believe that nature provides the perfect elements to nourish and sustain the health of the body’s largest organ, the skin.

Our intoxicating aromas are created with pure essential oils which lend incredible benefit to our products. The recipe is simple: Potent yet gentle botanicals and fruit derivatives combined with gentle base ingredients and pure essential oils. No nasty ingredients like fragrances or parabens. We are proud to offer simple, effective and dare we say delicious handmade body care products, all wrapped up in spiffy packaging. We love what we do and hope that you do too, enjoy!
