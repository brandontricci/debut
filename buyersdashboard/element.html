PHILOSOPHY

Welcome to Element Botanicals, Purveyors of Fine Handmade Bodycare We produce each product by hand in small batches with natural botanicals and essential oils, favoring Canadian and ethically sourced suppliers (...we source 100% of our ingredients from Canadian suppliers).
